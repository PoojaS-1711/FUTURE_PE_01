FUTURE_PE_01 AI WEBSITE PROMPT:

BUSINESS DETAILS:
- Business Name: [INSERT]
- Business Type: [INSERT — e.g. café, salon, clinic, agency, coaching center]
- Location: [INSERT CITY/AREA]
- Key Services: [INSERT 2-4 SERVICES]
- What Makes You Different (USP): [INSERT — what no other business in your area does]
- Target Customer: [INSERT — be specific, not "anyone"]
- Tone: [INSERT — friendly / professional / confident-simple]
- Trust Signal: [INSERT — e.g. "10 years in business", "500+ clients served", "award-winning"]
 
==========================================================

You are an expert website copywriter specializing in local businesses.
 
Using the business details above, generate complete website copy with four clearly separated sections. The copy must feel specific, human, and publish-ready — not generic or templated.
 
---
 
SECTION 1 — HOME PAGE COPY
 
Write the following in order:
 
1. HEADLINE: One punchy sentence. Lead with the customer's outcome, not the business name.
2. SUBHEADLINE: One to two sentences expanding on the headline. Clarify what the business does and for whom.
3. VALUE PROPOSITION: Three to four sentences. Answer why this business, why now, why it's different. Be specific — use the USP provided.
4. WHO IT'S FOR: Two to three sentences describing the ideal customer. Make them feel seen and understood.
5. KEY BENEFITS: Three distinct benefits written as short punchy statements. Focus on outcomes, not features.
6. SHORT INTRO SECTION: A natural 40-60 word paragraph that ties everything together and invites the reader to explore or take action.
 
---
 
SECTION 2 — SERVICE PAGE CONTENT
 
For each service listed, write:
 
1. SERVICE NAME: Clear and specific.
2. WHAT'S INCLUDED: Three to five bullet points describing exactly what the customer gets.
3. WHY CHOOSE US FOR THIS: Two to three sentences using the USP and trust signal to make this service feel like the obvious choice.
 
Repeat this block for each service provided.
 
---
 
SECTION 3 — CTA SECTIONS
 
Write three distinct CTA blocks:
 
1. PRIMARY CTA (top of page): A direct booking or contact prompt. One headline, one supporting sentence, one action button label.
2. TRUST-BUILDING CTA (mid page): Lead with the trust signal. Reassure hesitant customers. One headline, two supporting sentences, one action button label.
3. URGENCY/LOCAL CTA (bottom of page): Use the location and create mild urgency without being pushy. One headline, one supporting sentence, one action button label.
 
---
 
SECTION 4 — TONE GUIDE APPLIED
 
Confirm which tone was applied and provide:
- Three words that describe the voice used
- One sentence the business should NEVER sound like (wrong tone example)
- One sentence that perfectly represents their brand voice (right tone example)
 
---
 
RULES:
- Never use filler phrases like "we are passionate about" or "we are committed to excellence"
- Never start a headline with the business name
- Every section must feel written for THIS business specifically, not a generic template
- Use the location naturally — do not force it into every sentence
- Output sections with clear headers exactly as labeled above
